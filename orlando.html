What We Offer

Adult BJJ

Train with experienced instructors in a supportive environment. All skill levels welcome, from beginner to advanced competitors.

Kids BJJ

Build confidence, discipline, and self-defense skills while having fun. Age-appropriate classes for children 4-15 years old.

FR Fit

High-intensity fitness classes combining functional movements, strength training, and conditioning for all fitness levels.

MMA

Mixed Martial Arts training combining striking, grappling, and ground fighting. Develop well-rounded combat skills.

Muay Thai & Kickboxing

Master the art of eight limbs. High-intensity striking classes for fitness, technique, and competitive training.

Preschool BJJ Program

Fun, age-appropriate classes for ages 3-5. Building foundational skills through games and structured activities.

Self Defense Program

Practical self-defense techniques for real-world situations. Learn to protect yourself with confidence.

Women's Only BJJ

Empowering women-only classes focused on technique, self-defense, and fitness in a welcoming community.

Competition Team

Advanced training for athletes preparing for tournaments. Specialized coaching and competition strategies.

Our Coaches

Learn from experienced black belts dedicated to helping you reach your goals.

Professor Fabin Rosa

Head Instructor - 4th Degree Black Belt

Over 20 years of experience teaching Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu. Multiple-time champion and dedicated mentor focused on developing technical excellence.
