The Fabin Rosa Legacy

A lineage of champions, a tradition of excellence

Professor Fabin Rosa

Professor Fabin Rosa is a 4th Degree Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu Black Belt with over 20 years of experience in the martial arts. Born and raised in Brazil, Fabin began his journey in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu at a young age, training under some of the most renowned masters in the art.

His passion for BJJ brought him to the United States, where he dedicated himself to sharing the art with students of all ages and skill levels. Known for his technical precision and patient teaching style, Professor Fabin has developed countless champions and helped transform lives through the power of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu.

Today, Professor Fabin leads multiple locations across Central Florida, maintaining the highest standards of instruction while fostering a welcoming, family-oriented atmosphere where students can grow both on and off the mats.
